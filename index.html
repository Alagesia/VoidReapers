Who We Are

Not Just a Guild.
A Community.

The Void Reapers aren't defined by a single playstyle or content type. What sets us apart is something simpler and rarer: we actually play together.

No silent guild tabs. No strangers-who-happen-to-share-a-tag. We are a genuinely social guild where members connect, collaborate, and carry each other forward — whether you've been playing since launch or just installed the game.

Our entry barrier is deliberately low, but don't mistake that for low ambition. Some of our members grind operations or PvP daily at a high level. Others log on casually a few nights a week. Both are equally at home here.

Combat Module

Player vs Player

The Reaper Code

What We Stand For

Three principles that define every Void Reaper, whether you've been here a day or a year.

01 / 03

No One Plays Alone

We don't ghost each other in guild chat. If someone's looking for a group, we answer. That's the foundation everything else is built on.

02 / 03

All Levels, One Guild

Casual or hardcore, new recruit or veteran grinder — you belong here. We lift each other up rather than gatekeep. Your pace is your own.

03 / 03

Compete If You Want

The social vibe doesn't mean we shy away from competition. Guild prestige is real, rankings exist, and those who want to push limits will find others ready to go with them.
